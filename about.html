About IRCTC

Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation (IRCTC) is a subsidiary of Indian Railways that handles online ticketing, tourism, and catering services.

We aim to enhance passenger convenience by providing seamless railway ticket booking, exceptional tourism experiences, and high-quality catering services.

Our Mission

To revolutionize railway ticketing, tourism, and catering services through digital innovation and customer-centric solutions.

Our Vision

To be the leading digital service provider in railway travel, ensuring convenience, efficiency, and safety for millions of passengers.

Our Journey

IRCTC was established in 1999 to handle the catering and tourism operations of Indian Railways. Over the years, we have transformed railway ticketing in India, making travel more accessible and convenient for millions.
